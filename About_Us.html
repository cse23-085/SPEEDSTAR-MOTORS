About Speedstar Motors

Speedstar Motors is a family-owned auto repair shop that has been serving the community since 1995. We are committed to providing honest, reliable, and affordable services to keep our customers' vehicles running smoothly.

Our History

Speedstar Motors was founded in 1998 by John Smith. John was an automotive enthusiast who worked on cars with his dad growing up. After serving in the military, John decided to turn his passion into a business. What started as a small 2-bay garage has grown into a 8-bay shop with the latest diagnostic equipment and a team of expert technicians.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple - to provide top-notch service and repairs so you can feel confident in the safety and reliability of your vehicle. We treat every customer like family and always go the extra mile to ensure your satisfaction.

Meet Our Team

Our team of ASE-certified technicians have over 50 years of combined experience. We regularly attend training to stay up-to-date on the latest automotive technology. Our team loves cars and takes pride in the quality of our work.
